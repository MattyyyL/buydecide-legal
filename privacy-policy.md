# Privacy Policy for Buy Decide

**Last updated: 24 June 2026**

Buy Decide ("the App," "we," "us") is a mobile application that helps you make
purchase decisions. This policy explains what information the App handles and how.
We've tried to keep it plain-English.

**The short version:** Buy Decide has no accounts and no login. Your decisions, journal
entries, and settings stay **on your device**. Information leaves your device only when
you choose to use an AI feature, and to support ads and purchases. We do not sell your
personal information.

---

## 1. Who we are

Buy Decide is developed by **Matthew Lerantges** ("the developer").
Contact: **matthew03ler@gmail.com**.

## 2. Data stored on your device

Everything you create in the App is stored **locally on your device** and is not
transmitted to us:

- Your decisions (product names, your pros and cons, ratings, prices you enter, verdicts).
- Your product comparisons and cost-per-use calculations.
- Your Purchase Journal entries (what you bought or skipped and how you feel about it).
- App settings and usage counters.

This data is removed when you delete the relevant item in the App or uninstall the App.
We cannot access it and we keep no copy of it.

## 3. AI features (optional, only when you tap them)

The App offers optional AI-powered features ("Research with AI," "AI second opinion", "Which should I buy?"). These run **only when you choose to use them**. When you do, the App sends the following to our secure proxy server (hosted on Cloudflare), which forwards it to our AI and search providers to generate a response:

- The text you entered for that decision or comparison: product name(s), your pros/cons or wanted/avoided features, optional notes you type, the necessity/price values you entered, and your device's currency code.
- A short, anonymous summary of your Purchase Journal totals (counts only) when you have opted to factor your history into a decision.
- A randomly generated, anonymous installation identifier (a UUID created on your device) used solely to apply fair-use rate limits and abuse protection. It is **not** linked to your name, Apple ID, email, or any account, and is not the device's advertising ID.

**Providers.** Our proxy uses:

- **Anthropic** (the "Claude" AI model) to generate suggestions and opinions.
- **Serper.dev / Google Search** to fetch public review snippets used to ground the
  suggestions.

We do **not** send your saved history, journal details, or any contact information to these providers — only the specific decision text needed for the request you initiated. Our proxy stores only short-lived (about 2 days) anonymous request counters for abuse protection; it does not store the content of your requests. Our AI provider may process and briefly retain requests for safety and abuse-prevention purposes under its own policies. If you never use the AI features, **no decision data ever leaves your device**.

## 4. Advertising

The App displays ads through **Google AdMob**. To do this, AdMob may collect device and advertising identifiers and limited usage/diagnostic data to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's policies.

On Apple devices, the App will ask for your permission (via Apple's **App Tracking Transparency** prompt) before using your data for personalized advertising. If you decline, you will still see ads, but non-personalized ones, and **every feature of the App remains fully available**. You can change this any time in **iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**.

- Google's advertising privacy information: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
- AdMob: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

**Premium users:** purchasing the Pro upgrade removes all ads, so no ad-related data is collected for ad serving while ads are disabled.

## 5. Purchases

In-app purchases (the "Pro" upgrade) are processed by **Apple** and managed through **RevenueCat**, our subscription/purchase infrastructure provider. To validate and restore purchases, RevenueCat processes your App Store transaction/receipt information and assigns an anonymous app-user identifier. We do not receive your name or payment-card details;
Apple handles payment. See RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## 6. Information we do **not** collect

- We have **no user accounts** and require no login, name, or email to use the App.
- We use **no analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs** of our own.
- We do not knowingly collect precise location, contacts, photos, or health data.

## 7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country) and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

## 8. Your choices and rights

- **Delete your data:** remove items in the App, or uninstall it, to erase local data.
- **Advertising:** use the ATT prompt / iOS Tracking setting (Section 4), or go Pro to remove ads.
- Depending on where you live (e.g. EEA/UK GDPR, California CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Because we hold essentially no personal data about you on our servers, most data is already under your direct control on your device. For any request, contact us at the email above.

## 9. International transfers

Our AI proxy and providers (Cloudflare, Anthropic, Google) may process requests on servers located outside your country. By using the AI features you consent to this processing.

## 10. Data security

Decision data is stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard storage. AI requests are sent over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect information.

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, note them in the App or its store listing.
