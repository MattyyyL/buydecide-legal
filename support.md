## Frequently asked questions

**How does Buy Decide work?**
Tell it what you're thinking of buying, list your reasons for and against, rate how much
each matters, add your budget, and it gives you a clear "buy / don't buy" verdict. You can
also compare several products, work out a product's cost per use, and keep a Purchase Journal of how past buys worked out.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Your decisions, comparisons, and journal entries stay on your device — there's no
account and no login. Information only leaves your device if you choose to use an AI
feature, or to support ads and purchases. See our Privacy Policy: **https://mattyyyl.github.io/buydecide-legal/privacy-policy**.

**What does the Pro upgrade include?**
Pro removes all ads and unlocks unlimited AI research and comparisons plus full history
viewing. The free version includes a few AI uses per day so you can try everything.

**How do I restore my purchase / manage my subscription?**
Open the App's Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." To manage or cancel a subscription,
go to iOS **Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions**, or the App Store account settings.
Refunds are handled by Apple.

**The AI says "Too many requests."**
There's a fair-use limit to keep the service running for everyone. Wait a minute and try
again.

**Prices look wrong / in the wrong currency.**
The App uses your device's region to pick a currency. Check your iOS region in
**Settings → General → Language & Region**.

**How do I delete my data?**
Delete individual items in the App, or uninstall the App to remove everything stored
locally.
